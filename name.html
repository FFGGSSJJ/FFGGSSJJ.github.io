About My Name

My family name is Fu (‘傅’) and my given name is Guanshujie ('关抒杰'). My name consists of four characters in Chinese, which is longer than most of the people in China.

My father's family name is 'Fu' while my mother's is 'Guan', they put their family names together to get the first two characters of my name. Hence, 'Shujie' is actually my real given name.

Most of my Chinese friends will call me 'FuGuan' in case of my long name. I noticed that it might be somehow difficult for some native English speakers to pronounce 'Guan', you can then call me 'Shujie' for your convenience.
