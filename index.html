Research Interests

My current research interests fall on Computer Architecture/Computer System. I personally prefer this Quora answer to indicate some topics in Computer Architecture.

In my point of view, the state-of-the-arts CPU together with current system kernel may no longer be the perfect design to handle the explosively growing computation. During my experiences of In-storage Computing and In-network Computing, I found the reconfigurable devices might be the key for the creative scheme of future architecture design.

Hence, for now I am focusing on Edge Computing related research with smart hardware devices like DPU/FPGA/SmartSSD. I am working on several projects related to In-storage Computing and In-network Computing, advised by Prof. Nam Sung Kim, together with his Post-doc student and Ph.D. candidates.
